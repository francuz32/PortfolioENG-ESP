About Me

I'm a web designer, programmer, and translator in foreign languages from Chile.

I was born in 96 so my life has always been surrounded by technology and I've always been interested in things like electronics and videogames, along with foreign languages. I started learning English when I was 7 years old and then, in university, I learned Italian and some German.
